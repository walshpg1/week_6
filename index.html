About me

As a proud and dedicated member of Abbott since 2004, I have built a solid career at ADD Sligo, starting in the Kit-Pack Manufacturing department. Over the years, I have actively pursued further academic studies, which have empowered me to take on more advanced roles, leading to my current position. In 2023, I further enhanced my skills by enrolling in the National College of Ireland to study computer science, focusing on software development. Achieving this certificate enabled me to gain a deeper understanding of computer systems and contributed to my success in becoming a process specialist for both Core Diagnostics Longford and Sligo, supporting the design, validation, and implementation of POMS MES recipes. I am caring, hardworking, and responsible. I always aim to achieve high levels of compliance and integrity through my knowledge and expertise in this field. I am a straight-talking, pragmatic individual who enjoys getting to the root of issues promptly to achieve speedy resolutions and ensure high-quality, safe, and effective customer products
